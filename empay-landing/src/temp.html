Access Control

Four Roles. One System. Zero Overlap.

Precision-scoped permissions for every person in your organization — built with the principle of least privilege.

🛡️ Administrator
Full system control — the only role that can configure the entire platform.
  • ✅Create & manage all user accounts
  • ✅Assign & modify user roles
  • ✅View all dashboards & global reports
  • ✅Configure department structures
  • ✅Manage salary structures & pay grades
  • ✅Override payroll & leave decisions
  • ✅Access complete audit logs
  • ✅Generate organization-wide analytics
👤 Employee
Self-service access to personal data, attendance marking, and leave requests.
  • ✅Mark daily attendance
  • ✅Apply for leave requests
  • ✅View personal attendance history
  • ✅Download own payslips
  • ✅View own leave balance
  • ✅Update personal profile details
  • 🚫Cannot view other employee data
  • 🚫Cannot access payroll configurations
🗂️ HR Officer
Monitors workforce data, reviews attendance, and manages leave approvals across the org.
  • ✅View all employee attendance records
  • ✅Review & approve leave requests
  • ✅Reject leave with documented reason
  • ✅Generate HR-level reports
  • ✅Manage employee onboarding data
  • ✅View department-level analytics
  • 🚫Cannot process payroll or payrun
  • 🚫Cannot configure salary structures
💼 Payroll Officer
Owns the payrun lifecycle — from salary calculation to payslip generation and distribution.
  • ✅Initiate & process monthly payruns
  • ✅View attendance data for payroll calc
  • ✅Calculate PF, PT & net deductions
  • ✅Generate & distribute payslips
  • ✅View payroll history & run logs
  • ✅Export payroll data to reports
  • 🚫Cannot approve or reject leave
  • 🚫Cannot manage employee accounts

From onboarding to payslip — every step of your HR process, connected and automated.

02
Mark Attendance / Apply Leave

03
HR Reviews & Approves
HR Officer reviews requests and approves or flags issues
04
Payroll Officer Processes Payrun
Payroll Officer calculates salary, PF, PT and runs payroll
05
Payslip Generated & Distributed
Payslips auto-generated and available to each employee
